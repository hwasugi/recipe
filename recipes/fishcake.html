Eumok Bokkum (Stir-Fry Fishcake)

Prep : 15 min | Cook : 10 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

8 Sheets of Fish Cake
1/2 Onion
1/2 cup Carrots (julienned)
1 Scallion (oval sliced)
1 Korean Pepper (sliced)
3 TB Soy Sauce
2 TB Mirin
3 TB Brown Sugar
2 1/2 TB Minced Garlic
1/4 cup Water
2 TB Gochugaru (Red Pepper Flakes)
1 TB Sesame Oil
Pinch of Black Pepper

Garnish:
1 Red Chili Pepper (sliced)
Sesame Seeds

Instructions:

1.) Blanch fish cakes in hot water.
2.) Stir fry fish cakes until golden brown.
3.) Add carrots and onions then cook until softened.
4.) Add peppers, scallions, soy sauce, mirin, garlic, and water.
5.) Stir fry until cooked. 6.) Add gochugaru, sesame oil, and black pepper.
7.) Garnish.
