Pajeon

Prep : 15 min | Cook : 15 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

1 Cup Pajeon
1 1/4 Cup Water
1 Green Onion (oval sliced)
1 Egg
1/4 Cup Squid
2 Cups Chives (salted)

Optional:
1/3 Cup Carrots
Sliced Peppers
Kimchi

Instructions:

1.) Salt chives and wait 5 minutes.
2.) Add pajeon mix, water, green onion, egg, squid, and any optional ingredients.
3.) Oil pan and add mix.
