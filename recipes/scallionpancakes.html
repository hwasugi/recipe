Scallion Pancakes

Prep : 1 hr | Cook : 20 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1/2 tsp Salt
1/2 Cup Boiling Water
1/4 Cup Cool Water

Filling:
1/4 Cup + 2 TB All Purpose Flour
1/4 Cup Oil/Butter
3/4 tsp Salt
8~10 Green Onions (chopped)

Dipping Sauce:
1 1/2 TB Soy Sauce
1 TB Rice Vinegar
1/4 tsp Brown Sugar

Instructions:

1.) Combine flour and salt and stir.
2.) Slowly drizzle in hot water while mixing until water is fully absorbed.
3.) Slowly drizzle in cool water and stir until many flakes form.
4.) Press dough together and try to combine wet dough with dry flour.
5.) Knead into a ball and cover for 20 minutes.
6.) Filling: While dough is resting, combine flour, oil, and salt in a small bowl. Mix until smooth paste is formed.
7.) When dough is done resting, knead until smooth and cut dough into 6 even pieces. Cover the rest of dough with plastic wrap to avoid drying out and work dough one at a time.
8.) Roll each ball into a thin rectangle. Lift and turn dough regularly to prevent sticking.
9.) Spoon 1 TB of filling onto dough and spread evenly with the back of a spoon. Leave 1" on top and left end without filling.
10.) Sprinkle 2 TB green onions onto paste and concentrate most on one side of length and width.
11.) Roll dough from longer side of dough where the green onions are concentraed until it forms a tube. Lightly flatten tub and pinch side with more onions to seal.
12.) Take sealed side and pull and roll toward unsealed side. Tuck loose end under rolled bun. Seal pancake.
13.) Cover with plastic wrap. Repeat steps 8-12 until each pancake is ready.
14.) Roll each pancakes into a circle (freeze if desired with parchment paper dividing each pancake).
15.) On medium-high heat, add enough oil to fully coat the bottom. Fry each side until crisp and browned.
16.) Serve with dipping sauce (if desired).
