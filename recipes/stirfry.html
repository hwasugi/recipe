Stir Fry

Prep : 20 | Cook : 20

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

Pork
Onions
Carrots
Celery
Any other vegetables
Soy Sauce
Mirin
Sake
Brown Sugar
Garlic
Chili Powder
Turmeric
Noodles
Oyster Sauce
Salt/Pepper
Green Onion

Instructions:

1.) Cut meat into manageable chunks. Cut meat in thin slices against the grain.
2.) Marinate the meat with soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, garlic, chili powder, and turmeric.
3.) Cut up all vegetables in long slices (diagonally). Mince garlic
4.) Cook onions first. Put in half the vegetables and season. After they are mostly cooked, take them off the fire.
5.) Make sure the pan is hot enough. Cook the meat with garlic. Put in all vegetables and take off heat after fully cooked. 6.) Cook noodles separately. Add oyster sauce, salt, and pepper. 7.) Combine noodles, vegetables, and meat.
