Hotteok

Prep : 2 hrs | Cook : 20 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

1 Cup Warm Water
2 tsp Active Dry Yeast
1 TB Brown Sugar
1/2 tsp Salt
1 TB Oil
1 3/4 All Purpose Flour
1/2 Cup Glutinous Rice Flour (or 1/4 Cup All Purpose Flour)

Filling:
2/3 Cup Brown Sugar
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/3 Cup Sesame Seeds

Instructions:

1.) Mix brown sugar, cinnamon, and sesame seeds and set aside.
2.) Activate yeast by combining with warm water and sugar. Let sit for a few minutes.
3.) Add sea salt and oil, mix, then add the flours. Dough should stick to your fingers. Cover for 1 hour.
4.) Dough should rise and double in size. Knead dough and cover for 30 minutes. Dough should be less sticky.
5.) Sprinkle flour and knead dough to form a ball. 6.) Cut dough into 8 pieces and flatten them into 5 inches in diameter.
7.) Put filling in the middle and close by gethering edges and pinching until it seals.
8.) On medium-low heat, add oil to shallow fry the dough. Place seam side down and fry until golden.
9.) Flip and flatten with a spatula.
10.) Let cool for a few minutes and serve warm.
