Yo Fan

Prep : 20 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

3 Cups Sweet Rice
2 Dried Shiitake Mushrooms
2 Shallots (diced)
1 Strip of Pork (sliced)
Soy Sauce
Brown Sugar
Black Pepper
Mirin
Chinese Cooking Wine
Sesame Oil

Instructions:

1.) Wash sweet rice and soak overnight in the fridge. Soak mushrooms. Drain water from rice.
2.) Add mushroom water and sweet rice to rice pot and put water slightly less than 3 cups line (2 3/4).
3.) Drizzle sesame oil, sesame seeds, and rice vinegar.
4.) Cook pork on stove. Put on low heat, add shallots, mushrooms (diced), soy sauce, brown sugar, black pepper, mirin, chinese wine.
5.) Mix with sweet rice and drizzle sesame oil.
