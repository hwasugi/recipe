Spaghetti

Prep : 10 min | Cook : 20 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

1 1/2 Package Sauce
1 Onion (diced)
1 Package Italian Sausage
Basil Leaves
Spaghetti Noodles

Instructions:

1.) Sauté onions.
2.) Add Italian sausage and break apart.
3.) Add sauce after sausage is 80% cooked.
4.) Cover on low for 15 minutes. 5.) Add chopped basil as garnish
