Oi Kimchi (Spicy Cucumber Salad)

Prep : 20 min

--------------------------------------------------------

Ingredient List:

3 Cucumbers
Salt
1 Green Onion (chopped)
1 TB Garlic
2 TB Soy Sauce
1 TB Brown Sugar
1 TB Gochugaru (Red Pepper Flakes)
Sesame Oil
Sesame Seeds
Rice Vinegar or Sushi Vinegar

Instructions:

1.) Cut cucumbers chinese style (45 degree angle and rotate 45 degrees after every cut), salt, and flip.
2.) Wait 10 minutes for salt to be absorbed and dry with a paper towel.
3.) Add green onions, garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, and gochugaru.
4.) Drizzle sesame oil, sesame seeds, and rice vinegar.
